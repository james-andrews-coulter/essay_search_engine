Sexual Expectations

There’s an appealing but tragically unhelpful myth about sex and relationships: once we find the right partner, our sex life will be complete. We’ll at last settle into a comfortable, reliable (and sometimes very exciting) rhythm of good sex with someone we love who loves us back.

But in reality, getting together with someone long term is often the start of new and uncomfortable troubles around sex. Over time, three basic areas of distress can emerge:

•   There’s something that we really want to do in bed but we feel we can’t possibly ask our partner.

•   One of us has been unfaithful.

•   We rarely, if ever, have sex now.

Varieties of Friendship

We tend to think of friendship as a unitary category, but, in reality, there are a number of different kinds of friendship, each of which is specifically adapted to addressing a particular kind of loneliness. We might say that there are as many kinds of friend as there are ways of feeling isolated. What follows is a discussion of nine varieties of true friendship that track distinct ways in which two people can assist each other psychologically. We tease out the variants in a series of case studies.

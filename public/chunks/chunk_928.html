Classical vs Romantic

We may not be used to conceiving of ourselves in these terms, but the labels Romantic and Classical usefully bring into focus some of the central themes of how we think about love. Though they coexist to some extent in each of us, we all tend to be more one than the other. What follows are a few of the central contrasting characteristics of Romantic and Classical attitudes to love and relationships.

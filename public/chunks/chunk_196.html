This book is a fusion of my experiences with nature, formed since childhood, and a fifteen-year fascination with natural navigation, as well as the work of those whose writing has inspired me. I hope you will find much to inspire you in this book, and in the following ones too.

My second book, The Natural Explorer (Sceptre, 2012), expands on many of the themes in this book and also introduces a few new ones.

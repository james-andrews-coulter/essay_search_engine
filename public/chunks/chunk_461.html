A Less Pressured Life

As anxious, easily frightened people, we are likely to miss a crucial detail in the path to a calmer life: that is, that we cannot find calm simply on the basis of taking in a few ideas. In order to construct the quieter, less pressured lives we long for – lives in which we might be able to minimise contact with the sorts of events, people and opinions that torment and sadden us – we need also to alter our habits.

What might some of the elements of a less pressured existence look like?

PART I

Tags: nature, observation, attention, prioritization, mindfulness

PART I

Go for a three-minute walk outdoors. Note down the things your eyes are drawn towards.

If we have an understanding of the things our brain is likely to prioritize then we can temporarily override this pecking order and we will start to see new things.
